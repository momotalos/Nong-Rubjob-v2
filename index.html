Before we begin

Please review and agree to the following before using MindHub

🧠 This is an AI, not a clinician

N'Rub Job is an AI companion trained on clinical assessment frameworks (DSM-5, PHQ-9, GAD-7, Perceived Stress Scale). It is not a licensed therapist or psychiatrist and cannot diagnose, prescribe medication, or replace professional care. For acute crises, it will direct you to emergency services (1323, 1669, Samaritans 02-713-6793).

📊 What we collect from your conversations

Our AI analyzes your messages to derive clinical-style indicators (anonymized aggregates only):

  • Risk level (1–3) based on symptom patterns
  • Primary emotion & intensity (stress, anxiety, burnout, etc.)
  • Clinical screening scores (mood, anxiety, stress, sleep, energy)
  • Topic themes (workload, management, relationships — no specifics)
  • Assessment phase (which clinical step we're in)

🏢 How your company uses this data

Your employer sees only aggregated, anonymized organizational trends — never your raw messages or individual identity. Data is used to:

  • Identify organizational stress patterns (e.g., team-level burnout)
  • Inform workplace wellness policies and interventions
  • Connect you with EAP/professional support when risk is elevated

🔐 Your rights (PDPA/GDPR)

You may access, export, or delete all your data at any time from your account. You can withdraw consent and close your account at any point without affecting your employment. Chat history is stored locally on your device and encrypted at rest.

⚠ In crisis? If you are in immediate danger, please call emergency services (1669) or Thailand Mental Health Hotline 1323 directly — do not rely on AI alone.
